# TI-Dechange

A thematic, lightweight Terra Invicta mod that adds **positive** climate-recovery
narrative events to the late game.

Vanilla Terra Invicta punishes a warming world with floods, droughts, hurricanes,
and environmental degradation and once **Climate Change Mitigation** is researched,
it mostly just makes those disasters *less frequent*. Earth-Dechange fills the gap:
once humanity can begin repairing the climate, the recovering world tells its own
story of stabilization.

## How it works

As warming rises during the campaign, hidden recorders note the **peak severity**
the world reached.


After **Climate Change Mitigation**, as the global temperature anomaly *falls*,
the player walks through one of many variants per milestone at a time. Each step is a
distinct narrative event with its own text and modest rewards.

**Earned optimism:** a deeper crisis means a longer, harder journey but reaching
equilibrium from 2.5°C is more rewarding than from 1.5°C.
